Social Issues

Social issues are problems that affect many people, as they often have negative consequences for individuals, families, and communities. They usually arise from the aftermath of a natural event, inequality, and a history of conflict. Social issues include matters such as poverty, hunger,, and discrimination.

Environmental Issues

The earth needs your help to solve numerous environmental issues. Many of these issues stem from human activity such as deforestation, waste disposal, and pollution. As a result, the earth is gradually becoming warmer. Other effects include rising sea-level, melting glaciers, warming oceans, and increased humidity.

Economic Issues

Economic issues derive from an economy's limited resources. We must study how to allocate these scarce resources so that everyone's needs and desires are satisfied. The question becomes what, how, and for whom to produce goods and services. Issues include inflation, government spending, and corporate taxes.
